About Me

I'm a Aspiring Software Engineer | ECE Student

I am an Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE) student at VIT, driven by a passion for technology, innovation, and problem-solving. With hands-on experience in embedded systems, antenna design, and web development, I specialize in bridging the gap between hardware and software solutions. My technical skill set includes programming languages such as C, C++, Java, and Python, along with expertise in MATLAB, VHDL, and network design. I am proficient in web development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript) and have worked extensively with tools like Ansys for antenna simulation, Verilog for digital design, and Arduino/ESP32 for IoT-based projects. Beyond technical skills, I have contributed to multiple organizational initiatives, enhancing my leadership, teamwork, and project management abilities. I am always eager to embrace challenges and strive to develop impactful solutions through technology while continuously expanding my knowledge and skill set.
